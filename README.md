## 📈 Our "Problem to Profit" Operational Workflow

We keep our operations fully transparent with clients through unified automation:

1. **Discovery & Strategy:** Auditing a local merchant's physical stock, target demography, and logistics capability.
2. **The Build Phase:** Deploying a custom webapp/storefront while simultaneously configuring optimized seller channels.
3. **The Sales Engine:** Our in-house growth specialists launch highly targeted localized ad campaigns and manage live inbox closures.
4. **Automated Order Routing:** Orders captured by our team are automatically pushed to unified tracking sheets, prompting the client's warehouse/shop team to pack and ship via local couriers (Pathao, Steadfast, RedX).
5. **Data-Driven Scaling:** Analyzing monthly successful Cash-on-Delivery (COD) loops to fine-tune app features and scale advertising infrastructure.
